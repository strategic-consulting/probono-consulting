Pro Bono Consulting by Catherine Brys, PhD MBA

I offer pro bono consultancy services for social enterprises and charities in Scotland’s Central Belt.

Conflict prevention & conflict resolution

At times differences in opinion and approaches can lead to conflict which severely impacts the success of your organisation.
As a trained conflict resolution professional I can assist you in preventing situations from escalating or I can help you to resolve conflict situations.

  • Relationship issues within your organisation, e.g. divergent views between founders, partners or teams which impact the functioning of your organisation.
  • Relationship issues with partner organisations, clients or suppliers such as collaboration issues or late payment issues.

Consultation & facilitation

Organisational change often fails because of insufficient understanding of the different issues and perspectives and lack of buy-in from stakeholders.
I have extensive experience in designing effective consultation approaches. I can assist you to develop the most suitable and effective methodology for getting buy-in from your stakeholders, internal or external.

I can also facilitate discussions such as strategic reviews or discussions about succession planning.

Strategy

Reviewing and adapting your strategy periodically is essential for success. Not only does a well-thought through strategy provide a focus for your Team, it can also be an asset when you’re looking for parters or applying for funding.

  • For social enterprises in the start-up phase, I can help you to develop your strategy using a structured and flexible approach.
  • For established enterprises, I can support you with a strategy review or coach you in planning and executing a strategy refresh.
