Benefits

Complexity turned into clarity

Whatever the complexity of your organisation’s challenges, I will disentangle the strands and help you gain clarity. I will guide you to identify strategic options and to make decisions that will lead to more impact.

Fresh, neutral perspective

Involving a neutral, experienced consultant gives you an outside perspective. I have extensive experience in strategy, customer experience design and partnership working. Since I offer my services on a pro bono basis my only interest is to help you become more successful.

Practical solutions which you own

I will tap into my wide-ranging experience to help you challenge existing ways of thinking. I love creative problem-solving and will help you to arrive at solutions which will work for you and your stakeholders and which you will feel you own.
